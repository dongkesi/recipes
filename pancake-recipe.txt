Ingredients:
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 pumpkin
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the eggs, flour, pumpkin, water, baking soda
    and salt. Fully stirred.
 
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes until the color became golden yellow.
